Useful tips for your start.

How can I start it?

Hi If you have just started your new business, You do not need to worry about any things and all you need to do is to define your warehouse and add your product information or import them to the system. Maybe you have already run your business awhile or you are using a another management system and you plan to change it, So before you start, you would like to suggest you to initalize your inventory data.

How can I manage it?

When you start to use it, you must want this system is security for your business and all workers working on their duties. If so, you setup operation rule to your staff.

How can I check your sales result and profit?

Yes, this is most import things for all users. We offer the report funcitons,which enable you to quickly get them
